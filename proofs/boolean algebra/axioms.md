## Axioms
The set $B=\{0, 1\}$ will be used with two operations: $\lor$, and $\land$

> **Closure**
> $\forall\;a, b\in B$
> $$a\land b\in B$$
> $$a\lor b\in B$$

> **Cardinality**
> $1\not=0$

> **Commutative**
> $\forall\;a, b\in B$
> $$a\lor b = b\lor a$$
> $$a\land b = b\land a$$

> **Associative**
> $\forall\;a, b\in B$
> $$(a\lor b)\lor c = a\lor (b\lor c)$$
> $$(a\land b)\land c = a\land (b\land c)$$

> **Identity Element**
> $\forall\;a\in B$
> $a\lor 0=a$
> $a\land 1=a$

> **Distributive**
> $a\lor 0=a$
> $a\land 1=a$
